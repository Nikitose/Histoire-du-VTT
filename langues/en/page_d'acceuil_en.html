The riders of Paris

Let's start with the beginning. If the term did not yet exist, we nevertheless find traces of first all-terrain bikes in history in the mid-1950s within a French sports club! Which ?

The Parisian cross-club bike! It is made up of around twenty cycling enthusiasts from the Paris suburbs.who, between 1951 and 1956, developed bicycles that looked like mountain bikes. Riders equip in particular their suspension and fork mounts to protect against shocks. It was necessary think. But in 1956, everything stopped more or less suddenly. The pioneers take up motorcycles, or are leaving to do their military service according to what we know.

The Californian invention

We had to wait until the 1970s, this time in the United States, to see mountain biking reappear. A group of friends decided in turn to make a bike to descend any type of terrain in the County of Marin, California.

Their name is now well known in the world of cycling:

  • Joe Breeze
  • Charlie Kelly
  • Tom Ritchey
  • Gary Fischer
  • Otis Guy

Because these personalities played an essential role in the development of mountain biking, contributing to the design of bicycles adapted to off-road, to the creation of communities of off-road cyclists.

And this is how, in 1973, the “Mountain Bike” was born.
